The Games

Starting in 2014 the Five Nights at Freddy's games garnered widespread support across the globe. The franchise consists for 9 mainline games with 1 spin-off. The games consist of:

  • Five Nights at Freddy's (2014)
  • Five Nights at Freddy's 2 (2014)
  • Five Nights at Freddy's 3 (2015)
  • Five Nights at Freddy's 4 (2015)
  • Five Nights at Freddy's: Sister's Location (2016)
  • Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria Simulator (2017)
  • Ultimate Custom Night
  • Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted (2019)
  • Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach (2021)

    • Origin

    The games were created by Scott Cawthon in 2014. Originally exclusive to PC the games later came to consoles.
